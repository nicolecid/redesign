A Dream That Has Been Baking for Years

Baguette’s doors first opened in 1976 in Quito, Ecuador. With only one location, the bakery aimed to serve fresh breads, delicious pastries, and an unimaginable experience to every costumer. With its unique taste and quality, Baguette has quickly became part of the city because of their mission. With love and dedication, Baguette has grown to become the amazing bakery and dinner we know today.
